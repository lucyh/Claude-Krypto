KRYPTO

How to Play

Use your number cards to reach the Target using arithmetic — one step at a time.

  • Pick two numbers and an operator (+, −, ×, ÷).
  • The result replaces the two numbers you used.
  • Keep going until one card remains — and it equals the target!

Rules: results must be whole numbers and cannot be negative.

Stuck? Use Hint to see the next step, or Undo to backtrack.

Switch between 1–9 and 1–25 modes for different difficulty levels.
